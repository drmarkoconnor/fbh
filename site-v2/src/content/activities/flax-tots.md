Flax Tots gives local families an easy reason to gather, play and meet one another in the village.

Weekly class during term times.

It is a warm, sociable session for little ones and their grown-ups, with space to play, chat and enjoy a regular village get-together.  Drinks and snacks provided.



EVERYONE WELCOME.
