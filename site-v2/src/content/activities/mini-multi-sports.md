Mini Multi Sports is a child-centred coaching programme for younger children, with the emphasis on trying lots of sports rather than specialising too early.

Sessions focus on movement, balance, coordination, running, jumping, throwing and catching, while keeping the experience enjoyable and energetic for children and families.
