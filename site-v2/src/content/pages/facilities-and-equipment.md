This guide brings the most useful parts of the facilities list into the site so hirers can scan it more easily before they book.

## What is available as standard

- Main hall with a permanently installed portable stage.
- Kitchen with fridges, microwave, kettles, oven, hob and grill.
- 14 trestle tables in the hall store, including 4 lower tables better suited to children.
- Additional round tables, chairs and serving stock available from storage when arranged in advance.

## Furniture and event setup

- **Tables in the hall store:** 10 full-size trestle tables and 4 lower tables better suited to children.
- **Extra tables:** 12 large round tables are stored separately and should be requested in advance.
- **Chairs:** 45 chairs are kept in the hall, with additional chairs available in storage if you let the bookings team know beforehand.
- **Stage:** the portable stage is permanently installed at the end of the hall.

<div class="note-card stack">
  <h3>Advance notice helps on the day.</h3>

## Housekeeping notes

- Linen is **not** provided, including tablecloths, dish cloths and tea towels.
- The Hub does **not** provide rubbish or recycling facilities, so all waste must be taken away at the end of your hire.
- Please report any breakages or damage to [bookings@flaxbourtonhub.org](mailto:bookings@flaxbourtonhub.org).
