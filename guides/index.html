Stage Sound and Lights

  • Both systems are turned on and off via plugs at the back of the stage
  • Please ensure you turn off both when you finish
  • If you need to turn off the sound, please ensure you wait 10 seconds before turning back on

Lighting

  • The lights are controlled via foot pedals
  • Please use these to setup the correct lighting for your booking
  • Note that there are disco and flat modes and a variety of colours

Sound

  • You can link in devices via Bluetooth connecting to Music BT passcode 0000
  • There are cables that can link into headphone sockets in the cabinet and outside
  • There is a radio mic, please ensure this is turned off before you leave as the batteries will drain otherwise
  • You can link mixing desks on the external sockets and also instruments and mics
  • Volumes are controlled via the mixing desk
  • Please be mindful of the neighbors and do not have the music excessively loud
